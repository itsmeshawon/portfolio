History of SBE

SBE is the Bangladesh’s largest domestic manufacturer of hygienic process systems. The aim of the company is to produce high quality products at a fair price.

Established in 2013 as a joint venture company between Bioengineering, AMS Technology, Incepta Pharmaceuticals. Swiss and German know-how in the field of process and production technology, following the highest quality standards, coupled with the urge to open up new business opportunities, resolute to learn new things, focused to improve and perfect, are the best possible conditions for the success of the young company with its highly motivated team.

Who We Are

Swiss Biohygienic Equipment is an equipment manufacturer that specializes in the field of hygienic plant and process pipe construction. Water and steam generation as well as distribution systems form the core products.
