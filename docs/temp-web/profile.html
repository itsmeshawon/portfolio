Mahedi Salim

A hybrid product person, ambivert, and behavioral strategist who helps companies design profitable digital products.

After spending an era doing UX/UI, data visualization, and digital design–my main focus is now behavioral strategy, where I help companies convert and retain more users by analyzing the psychology of a product or experience–not the ‘look’ of it.

I am passionate about my profession, I do UX, UI or visual design as well as other design things. During the journey of a project, I could be involved from the beginning, doing things like planning and road mapping, to doing customer research when building a feature I have to think about what features the app will have, what will it make stand out from competitors, what are the most critical features that need to be done, what other features can be added in the future, how will they work, how would it connect to other company products. To the visual side, I do highfidelity mockups and prototyping, through to the implementation phase, passing off assets to the engineers and QA.

When I'm not at my computer, you might find me cooking biryani or experimenting with some new recipes, listening to random music channels at AccuRadio, or exploring new cafes and restaurants on my quest to find something delicious in the street of Dhaka.
